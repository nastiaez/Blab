# Closed-test invite (Step 3.6)

Play requires **12+ testers opted in for 14 continuous days** before you can
go to production. Send this to friends/family. The 14-day clock starts once
they've joined, so recruit early.

---

## How to set it up (your side, Play Console)

1. Play Console → your app → **Testing → Closed testing** → create a track.
2. Add testers by email (a Google Group or an email list of 12+).
3. Copy the **opt-in link** Play gives you.
4. Send the message below with that link.

---

## Message to send testers

> Hey! I built a little app called **Blab** — you chat with a friend and it
> translates every message so you both pick up each other's language as you
> go. I'm running a 2-week test before launch and need a few people on it.
>
> Two quick steps:
> 1. On your Android phone, tap this link and accept: **[opt-in link]**
> 2. Then install Blab from the Play Store link it shows you.
>
> After that just message me inside the app once or twice over the next two
> weeks — that's all Google needs to see. Thanks a ton 🙏
