Both buttons same style: white background + border. Apple gets slightly darker border (brand req). Clean, no hierarchy between them. Used by most B2B and design-forward apps.

B — Platform colors (Apple black, Google outlined)

Apple button is black filled (Apple's official brand guideline). Google stays white + border. Creates a visual hierarchy — Apple feels more prominent. Used by Notion, Superhuman, many iOS-first apps.

C — Side-by-side icon pills (iOS), full-width (Android)

On iOS: Apple + Google sit side by side as compact pills — saves vertical space for the email form below. On Android: Google expands to full width. Note: Apple's brand guidelines technically require "Continue with Apple" text, so this risks App Store rejection.
