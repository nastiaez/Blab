Who is Blab primarily for?

The spec supports couples AND friends — but the brand needs to speak to someone specific. Who's the heart of it?

A

Romantic Partners

Long-distance couples, international relationships. Learning their language is an act of love. Emotional, intimate undertone.

B

Close Friends

Besties, pen pals, online friends across borders. Social, fun, no pressure. Language is how you vibe together.

C

Anyone With a Real Connection

Couples, friends, family — all of the above. Brand doesn't pick sides. The hook is "people who actually know each other," not the relationship type.
