How should Blab make users feel?

Pick the emotional core of the brand. This shapes everything — color, type, tone of voice.

A

Warm & Intimate

Like texting someone you love. Cozy, personal, low-pressure. Learning feels like a side effect of connection, not the point.

B

Playful & Energetic

Fun, a little cheeky, light. Talking to someone new is exciting. The name "Blab" already hints at this — expressive, talkative, spontaneous.

C

Cool & Modern

Minimal, clean, sharp. Feels like a premium messaging app. Language learning is invisible — just a sleek communication tool.

D

Confident & Empowering

You're becoming someone who speaks another language. Bold, motivating, a sense of growth and achievement.
