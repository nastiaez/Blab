Logo — what's your idea?

Tell me in the terminal. Sketch, word, mood, reference — whatever you have. I'll sanity-check it against Witty + Bold and we'll plan execution.

What I need from you
  • Concept — what's the visual idea? (a letterform? a symbol? a mark? something abstract?)
  • Why it — what does it represent? (the conversation? the bridge between languages? "Blab" as a word?)
  • Tool — what are you designing it in? (paper sketch / Figma / iPad / something else)
What good looks like for Witty + Bold

Aim for

  • One strong idea, not two stacked
  • Confident shape — readable at 24px (app icon)
  • Some character/quirk — geometric twist, custom letter, expressive curve
  • Works in 1 color
  • Memorable in 3 seconds

Avoid

  • Speech bubbles (everyone does it)
  • Globe + flags (cliché for language apps)
  • Cute mascot (off-brand for Bold)
  • Gradient-only marks (won't survive monochrome)
  • Generic sans wordmark with no twist
