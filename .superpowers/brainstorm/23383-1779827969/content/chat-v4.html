Popup spec
  • Trigger: tap any word in either language
  • Position: floats over the tapped word, tail-arrow points at it. Clamped to phone bounds (FR-12)
  • No scrim — rest of chat stays readable
  • Layout (left-aligned): top row = original word + speaker icon · transliteration line · divider · translation
  • Type: all normal weight, no italics
  • Dismiss: tap outside, or tap another word to move the popup
