Speaker icon — pick one

Each shown as it would appear in the popup. Same size, same color (terracotta). Yes — pick one and we replace every instance everywhere.

My read

A (speaker + waves) is the safest — every user already knows it means "audio." B (waves only) is the quietest and most modern — pairs best with the quiet-bold register. D (mouth) is the most ownable and on-brand for a talk app — risk is it's less instantly recognizable. E (text pill) is the most on-brand voice but takes more space.

Note on "replace everywhere"

Yes — once locked, this icon becomes the only audio glyph in the app. Same SVG in the popup, message bubble (FR-13), profile pronunciation samples, anywhere TTS is offered. One source of truth — change once, change everywhere.
